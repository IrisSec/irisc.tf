IrisSec

IrisSec is a group of nerds who love breaking computers and making stuff. We host IrisCTF, an annual 48-hour CTF that takes place the first full weekend of every year.

Announcement

IrisCTF 2025 will be taking place from 4 January 2025 @ 00:00 UTC to 6 January 2025 @ 00:00 UTC. Registration will open in December at 2025.irisc.tf.
